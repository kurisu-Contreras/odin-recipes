Fried Egg

Ingredients

  • A beautiful egg but careful, because the candy eggs not count okay?
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • black pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. Put the frying pan in the stove
  2. Take the olive oil and put it in the frying pan
  3. Put a bit of salt in the frying pan
  4. This is accord to you taste, i prefer wait five minutes and then put the egg in the frying pan
  5. Put a bit of black pepper
  6. Enjoy !
